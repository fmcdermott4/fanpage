Dethklok is the world's greatest culture force. Their first album, "Dethwater" was recorded in a submarine deep in the Mariana Trench and was only meant to be heard by fish. Some fans listened to "Go to the Water" and took the lyrics as actual instructions, drowning themselves. Because of the controversy surrounding the album, Dethklok did not perform it live for a very long time. Two later albums were later created by Dethklok. Their second album was not named, and the album artwork featured the band members shirtless with extreme physique. Their third album was entitled "Seething Vortex."
